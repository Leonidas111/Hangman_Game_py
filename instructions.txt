
The instructions for the Hangman game are as follows:

1) enter the start game option
2) when you start the game you must enter only 1 letter
3) when you start you have 6 lives
4) for each error 1 life is subtracted

NOTE: THE WORDS ARE 100% RANDOM, YOU HAVE TO GUESS THEM
OBSERVE THE RULES AND ENJOY THE GAME OF THE HANGMAN!
